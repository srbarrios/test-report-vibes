# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any behavior that would reasonably make others feel unsafe

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy. They may remove or edit comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that violate these standards.

## Reporting concerns

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to `srbarrios@gmail.com` or GitHub `@srbarrios`.
